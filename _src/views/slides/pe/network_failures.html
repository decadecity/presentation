Network Connectivity

One of the obvious answers is network connectivity. Not just flaky mobile networks but also infrastructure. Serving JavaScript from a CDN is widely regarded as a best practice - and with good reason. So who here works on a site where the JavaScript is served from a CDN? >>>> CDNs, proxies, caches and pretty much any other networking infrastructure that your site travels through are all potential points of failure and there is nothing you can do to prevent them failing.
