JavaScript disabled?

0.2%

…or just not available?

0.9%

Gov.uk carried out some research into the availability of JavaScript and found that 0.2% of people had JavaScript disabled. But that's not the whole story. >>> The really interesting thing here is that whilst only 0.2% of people didn't have JavaScript enabled - they also found that for 0.9% of people JavaScript had failed for some other reason. So what could these reasons be and what can we do about them?
