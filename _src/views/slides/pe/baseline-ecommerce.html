E-commerce Baseline

  1. Find a product
  2. Add it to your basket
  3. Delivery instructions
  4. Pay
Lets take the example of an e-commerce site, it's a familiar concept and we all probably use this flow at least on a daily basis. 1. find a product 2. add it to your basket 3. add delivery instructions 4. pay That's the minimum, you can't take one of those steps away and still be viable as an e-commerse business. Note the things that aren't in that list: * Registration - you have taken enough information to fulfil the order, you don't need them to have a user account. * Upsell - if you don't make the sale in the first place then you can't upsell. * Interactive 3D virtual product tour - yeah, sure that looks pretty but if they can't purchase it then what's the point?
