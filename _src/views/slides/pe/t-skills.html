T-shaped skills

I'm going to take a slight digression here and talk about the T-shaped skills model. This refers to how people's skills are typically structured. The vertical stem represents deep knowledge in a narrow subject area. This is your specialism, this is what you're best at. The horizontal bar represents a shallow knowledge of a broad range of subjects. This is what enables you to understand how your specialism best fits within a wider context. This is what makes your specialism useful.
